Why Gold Price Changes Every Day?

Gold prices are not fixed. They change continuously due to several global factors. The most important factor is the US Dollar value. When the dollar becomes strong, gold prices usually drop, and when the dollar weakens, gold prices increase. Other factors include inflation, interest rates, global economic stability, and central bank policies. During economic uncertainty, investors move their money into gold, which increases demand and price.

Gold as a Safe Investment

Gold is considered a "safe-haven asset" because it maintains its value during financial crises. Unlike paper currency, gold cannot be printed or devalued easily. This makes it a preferred investment for long-term wealth protection. Many investors use gold to balance their investment portfolios and reduce risk.

24K, 22K and 18K Gold Explained

24K gold is the purest form of gold with 99.9% purity. It is mostly used for investment purposes. 22K gold contains a small percentage of other metals, making it slightly more durable and commonly used in jewelry. 18K gold has even more alloy content and is used in modern jewelry designs.

Gold Price in Pakistan

In Pakistan, gold prices are influenced by international market rates and local currency exchange rates (PKR vs USD). Any change in global gold price directly affects the local gold rate. That is why live tracking is important for buyers and investors in Pakistan.
